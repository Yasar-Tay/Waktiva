# Privacy Policy for Waktiva

**Last Updated:** February 24, 2025

Waktiva is an open-source, privacy-focused prayer times application. We believe that your private life and religious practices should remain private. This Privacy Policy explains our approach to data and why you can trust Waktiva.

## 1. No Data Collection
Waktiva **does not collect, store, or share** any personal information. 
- We do not have servers to store your data.
- We do not require account creation or registration.
- We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Adjust, etc.).

## 2. Location Data
To provide accurate prayer times and Qibla direction, Waktiva requires access to your device's location.
- **Local Processing:** Your location data is processed **locally on your device**. 
- **No Tracking:** We do not track your movement or store your location history.
- **Offline Capability:** Waktiva can download prayer times for up to three months so you can use the app without an active internet connection.

## 3. No Advertisements
Waktiva is **100% ad-free**. We do not use any advertising networks, which means no ad-related tracking or profiling occurs within the app.

## 4. Third-Party Services
Waktiva uses the following services to function:
- **Al-Adhan API:** Used to fetch prayer time data. No personal identifiers are sent to this service; only the coordinates or city name required for calculation.
- **Google Play Billing:** If you choose to support the project via a donation, the transaction is handled securely by Google Play. Waktiva does not see or store your payment details.
- **MapLibre/OSM:** Used for the Qibla map feature. Map tiles are fetched from open providers.

## 5. Open Source Transparency
Waktiva is open-source. This means anyone can inspect our code to verify that we handle data exactly as described in this policy. You can find our source code here: [https://github.com/Yasar-Tay/Waktiva.git](https://github.com/Yasar-Tay/Waktiva.git)

## 6. Permissions
- **Location:** Used for prayer times and Qibla.
- **Notifications:** Used to alert you of prayer times.
- **Exact Alarm:** Used to ensure the Adhan sounds exactly on time.

## 7. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
