## Long Description (Max 4000 characters)

**Waktiva** is a modern, high-precision prayer times application designed with modern weather apps look and privacy in mind. Built for the global Muslim community, it offers a beautiful, ad-free experience without any tracking.

### 🕋 Accurate Prayer Times
Never miss a prayer. Waktiva uses advanced location-based calculations to provide precise timings for Fajr, Sunrise, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha. 
- **Proactive Caching:** Fetches up to 90 days of data in advance so you stay updated even offline.
- **Multiple Methods:** Choose from Diyanet (Turkey), MWL, ISNA, Egypt, Makkah, Karachi, and more.
- **Madhab Support:** Configure Shafi/Standard or Hanafi calculation for Asr.

### 🧭 Advanced Qibla Finder
Find the direction of the Kaaba with ease using our dual-mode Qibla locator:
- **Compass View:** A responsive, high-precision compass.
- **Map View:** Find Qibla direction on a satellite or standard map using MapLibre integration. Afterall we all have experienced Phone sensors are sometimes enough.

### 🔊 Customizable Adhan Notifications
Personalize your call to prayer with deep customization options:
- **Individual Sounds:** Set different Adhan audio for each prayer time.
- **Custom Audio Support:** Upload your own MP3 or WAV files.
- **Pre-Adhan Warnings:** Get a gentle notification minutes before prayer time to help you prepare for Adhan.
- **Mute Toggle:** Quickly skip the Adhan for the next prayer if you're in a meeting or masjid.

### 🌙 Moon Phase & Hijri Calendar
- **Celestial Visualization:** A moon phase view with realistic textures and atmospheric effects.
- **Hijri Date:** Stay connected with the Islamic calendar.
- **Religious Days:** Built-in list of significant dates like Ramadan, Eid al-Fitr, Eid al-Adha, and more.

### 🛠️ Reliability & System Health
Unlike other apps where notifications often fail, Waktiva proactively monitors your device settings:
- Detects if Battery Optimization or Do Not Disturb might block your Adhan.
- Uses Exact Alarms (Android 14 ready) to ensure punctuality.
- Guidance on fixing system-level issues for 100% reliability.

### 🌍 Multi-Language Support
Available in English, Türkçe, العربية, Français, Deutsch, Español, Italiano, Bahasa Indonesia, اردو, বাংলা, فارسی, Bahasa Melayu, Русский, Shqip, Bosanski, Nederlands, Suomi, Polski, Português, and Svenska. Full RTL support for a native experience.

### 🔒 Privacy-Focused & Open Source
- **No Ads:** Completely free of distractions.
- **No Tracking:** We don't collect your data or sell your location.
- **Privacy First:** We only request permissions necessary for core functionality.
